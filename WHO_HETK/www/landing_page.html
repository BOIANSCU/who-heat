Welcome to the Health Equity Assessment Toolkit (HEAT)!

The Health Equity Assessment Toolkit enables the exploration and comparison of within-country health inequalities across countries. Assessing health inequalities guides evidence-based priority setting for defining equity-oriented health interventions.

You can explore inequalities in a country of interest, and then compare inequalities in the selected country with the situation in other countries. Inequalities can be assessed using disaggregated data and summary measures. Interactive tables and graphs reveal many facets of inequalities in a comprehensive and dynamic way.

HEAT is linked to the WHO Health Equity Monitor database. Data for Morocco from 2011 was provided by the country. Alternatively, data can be uploaded in a pre-defined format (please refer to the template). To get started, select the Explore Inequality tab and use the selection menu on the left in the Disaggregated data – tables or Disaggregated data – graphs tab to fetch data. Continue by exploring inequalities using summary measures, and comparing within-country inequalities across countries.
